  • Additional Notes:
    • Quantities also include negative values due to cancellations.
    • There are orders that are directly followed by their cancellation, for example the order with the minimum and maximum quantity
      • This should be corrected when cleaning the data, at least within a 14 day time window (UK's cooling-off period).
    • To generate a aggregated time series, the total price needs to be calculated (quantity * price).

3. Purchase Prediction Feature Analysis¶

The main goal is to figure out if there is any further feature engineering or preparation needed.

3.1. General info about the data:¶

The data set generated by the PurchasePredictionPreprocessor contains a feature set of five features, including

  • recency: How recent is the last purchase in days (starting from the end of the defined timespan).
  • frequency: How often did the customer purchase over the defined timespan.
  • diff_purchase_t_1: Difference in days between the last purchase and the second last purchase.
  • diff_purchase_t_2: Difference in days between the last purchase and the third last purchase.
  • purchase_subsequent_quarters: How often did the customer purchase in two subsequent quarters.

The training data was calulate only for the quarters prior to the last one (2009-12-01 - 2011-09-30). The last quarter was used to generate the target labels (y_label), where the label 1.0 defines that the customer will purchases next quarter and the label 0.0 that the customer won't purchase next quarter. To make predctions with a model, the features are calculated over the whole time span (including the last quarter).
